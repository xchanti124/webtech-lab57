Anime Archive

Purpose of this website

Anime Archive is a website that is supposed to be an archive of anime (japanese animation series) where anyone can add their favorite anime and help expand the website. The anime can be added with a poster that depicts the anime, the release year, the genre that it is part of and a description of the plot of the anime. Eventually, this can become a space where everyone can come together and share their favorite anime with other people and describe the anime in their own words for everyone else to see. If you want, you can check out some cool other websites that are great for learning more about anime!

  • Crunchyroll
  • My Anime List
  • Aniwave
  • Anime Music Quiz
  • Jump to archive!
