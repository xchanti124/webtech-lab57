Anime Archive

Purpose of this website

Anime Archive is a website that is supposed to be an archive of anime (japanese animation series) where anyone can add their favorite anime and help expand the website.
The anime can be added with a poster that depicts the anime, the release year, the genre that it is part of and a description of the plot of the anime.

Eventually, this can become a space where everyone can come together and share their favorite anime with other people and describe the 
anime in their own words for everyone else to see. If you want, you can check out some cool other websites that are great for learning
more about anime!
  • Crunchyroll
  • My Anime List
  • Aniwave
  • Anime Music Quiz
  • Jump to archive!

Website navigation

For easier and better navigation, there is a table of contents with the titles of the anime that 
are contained in the archive, so if you want to jump to a specific anime from this list, you can click
on the name and access all the information about it.

Cowboy Bebop
カウボーイビバップ
 1998
  • Anime
  • Cyberpunk
  • Action

Japanese animated television series set in 2071 and centered on the adventures of a gang of bounty hunters in space, the story delves into the unresolved issues of the protagonists' past, exploring concepts such as existentialism, boredom, loneliness, and the influence of the past.

Psycho Pass
サイコパス
 2012
  • Thriller
  • Anime
  • Cyberpunk

Psycho Pass is a psychological thriller anime that plays in an authoritarian future dystopia, where there is a system that can predict your likelihood of becoming a criminal and preventatively isolate you from society when your "psycho pass" (criminal likelihood score) is over a certain limit.

Jujutsu Kaisen
呪術廻戦
 2018
  • Anime
  • Adventure Fiction
  • Supernatural Fiction
  • Shounen

Jujutsu Kaisen is about a high school student Yuji Itadori who joins a school club during which he and his friends summon cursed creatures which ends up in Yuji's adventures and him fighting cursed creatures.

Attack on Titan
進撃の巨人
 2013
  • Anime
  • Action
  • Adventure

Attack on Titan is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. It is set in a world where humanity is forced to live in cities surrounded by three enormous walls that protect them from gigantic man-eating humanoids referred to as Titans; the story follows Eren Yeager, who vows to exterminate the Titans after they bring about the destruction of his hometown and the death of his mother.

Monster
モンスター
 2004
  • Anime
  • Crime
  • Mistery

Monster is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Naoki Urasawa. The story revolves around Kenzo Tenma, a Japanese surgeon living in Düsseldorf, Germany whose life enters turmoil after he gets himself involved with Johan Liebert, one of his former patients, who is revealed to be a psychopathic serial killer.
